What this demonstrates

Architecture as sampled geometry

This procedural interpretation samples a tapered, faceted tower rather than loading a survey scan. Its split apex and changing façade profile evoke The Shard while demonstrating architectural sampling, rotation matrices, perspective projection, and depth sorting.
